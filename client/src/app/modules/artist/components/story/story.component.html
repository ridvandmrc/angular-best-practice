Drake

Aubrey Drake Graham is a Canadian rapper and singer. An influential figure in contemporary popular music, he has been credited with popularizing R&B sensibilities in modern hip hop. Gaining recognition by starring as Jimmy Brooks in the CTV teen drama series Degrassi: The Next Generation, Drake pursued a recording career in 2006, releasing his debut mixtape Room for Improvement.
